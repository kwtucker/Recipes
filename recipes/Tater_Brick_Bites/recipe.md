# Tater Brick Bites

![Tater Brick Bites](images/taterbrickbites.png)

- Time: 30 min
- Yield: 4 to 6 servings
- Source: [Tater Brick Bites](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KATgzM8fYmo)

## Ingreients

- Potatos about 3
- Butter | 4 tablespoons unsalted |
- Cajun seasoning
- Honey
- BBQ Sauce
- Dijon Mustard
- Chosen Foods Mayo
- Chosen Foods Avacodo spray to coat the pan.
- Green onions

## Lets Go

### Potatos
1. Preheat the oven to 425 F.
1. Clean and slice potatos into slabs about a 1/4 thick.
1. Use two skewers or chopsticks to cross hatch both sides of the slices. Makes those cuts on both sides without cutting them in half.
1. Soak potatos in ice bath to draw out the starch.
1. Melt the butter and mix in the cajun seasoning.
1. Dry and place potatos in the coated pan.
1. Paint on the butter mix on potatos.
1. Put them in the oven for 30 minutes.

### Sauce
1. Whisk together the honey, BBQ, dijon, chosen food mayo in a bowl.

### Garnish
1. Chop green onions for some color and flavor.
